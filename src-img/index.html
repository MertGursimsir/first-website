The ancient Greeks once said: "Everything was made up of 4 elements which are earth, water, air and fire."

Allegories of the classical elements, by Giuseppe Arcimboldo.

The first element I want to talk about is (as might be expected) fire.

ELEMENT TEMPERAMENT MYSTIC CHARACTER GENERAL
fire gall, yellow emotional, irresistable, mercurial, unpredictable desire and love

Fire is both hot and dry. It closely linked to air. Fire needs 3 things in order to exist: oxygen, fuel and heat.

Fire can be heard, felt, seen and it creates light, heat and smoke.

Fire might seem dangerous and not safe and also in Japanese traditions, fire represented things that destroy. Although, fire is one of the most essential things in the world. The control of fire by early humans was a turning point in the technological evolution of humans because fire provided a source of warmth, protection from predators, a way to create more advanced hunting tools, and a method for cooking food.

A diorama showing Homo erectus.

All in all, fire has been using by early modern humans for approximately 200,000 years and it is one of the reasons of life.

So we can learn so many things from everything even though they are dangerous.
